Competencies

What Students Can Do
Competency 1

Build professional Excel workbooks: create, structure, navigate, and manage organized workbooks that follow business conventions.

Competency 2

Produce business-ready analytical worksheets: enter and clean data, apply formulas, format clearly, and configure print-ready layouts for stakeholder review.
